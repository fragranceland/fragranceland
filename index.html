Some common questions
were often asked

How do I choose the right perfume for me?

Consider your personal style, favorite scents, and the occasion. Floral and fruity notes are great for daytime, while woody and musky scents suit evening wear. You can also try samples to find the perfect match.

How long does perfume last on the skin?

It depends on the fragrance type and your skin type. Eau de Parfum can last 6-8 hours, while Eau de Toilette lasts about 3-5 hours. Applying to pulse points can extend longevity.

Where should I apply perfume for the best results?

Apply to pulse points like the wrists, neck, behind the ears, and inner elbows. These warm areas help diffuse the fragrance throughout the day.

Can perfume expire?

Yes, most perfumes last 3-5 years. Changes in color or scent indicate it might be time to replace it.

Do you offer samples before purchase?

Yes, we offer sample sizes for selected perfumes so you can try them before committing to a full-size bottle.

How can I make my perfume last longer?

Apply perfume to moisturized skin, especially after using unscented lotion. Spraying on pulse points and lightly misting clothes can also help the scent last longer.
